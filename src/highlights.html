Club Highlights & Achievements

Celebrating the milestones, victories, and outstanding accomplishments of UCP student clubs across all categories.

Major Achievements

Our clubs have represented UCP on regional, national, and international stages.

🥇
National Debate Championship 2025

UCP Debate Society — 1st Place

The UCP Debate Society clinched first place at the National Intercollegiate Debate Championship, defeating 24 universities in the final round held at LUMS, Lahore.

🏆
NASCON Hackathon 2025

Coding Society — Best Innovation Award

A four-member team from the Coding Society won the Best Innovation Award at NASCON for developing an AI-powered campus navigation system using computer vision.

🥈
Punjab University Sports Fest 2025

Cricket Club — Runners Up

The UCP Cricket Club reached the final of the inter-university sports fest, finishing as runners-up after a thrilling last-over contest against University of Engineering & Technology.

🎭
All Pakistan Drama Festival 2025

Drama Society — Best Direction Award

The Drama Society's original play "Khwab aur Khak" earned the Best Direction Award at the All Pakistan Drama Festival, receiving praise for its powerful storytelling and staging.

📸
International Youth Photography Contest 2024

Photography Club — Top 10 Finalist

Two UCP Photography Club members were selected as Top 10 finalists in the Global Youth Photography Contest, with their work exhibited in Karachi and online.

🤖
Pak-China Robotics Challenge 2024

Robotics Club — Special Recognition

UCP Robotics Club received a Special Recognition award at the Pak-China Robotics Challenge for their autonomous line-following bot project, which was among the top 5 designs.

Club Milestones

A brief history of growth and achievements since clubs were established at UCP.

2019

First Student Clubs Established

The Coding Society and Drama Society became the first two officially recognised student clubs at UCP, with a combined membership of 45 students.

2021

First National-Level Win

UCP Debate Society won the Northern Regional Debate Championship for the first time, putting the university on the national debate map.

2022

Clubs Portal Launched

The Student Affairs Office launched this online portal to centralise information about all clubs, events, and membership across UCP.

2023

20 Clubs Milestone

UCP reached 20 officially registered student clubs, with combined membership exceeding 1,000 students for the first time.

2025

National Championship Victory

The debate team won the National Intercollegiate Championship — the highest competitive achievement in UCP clubs history to date.
